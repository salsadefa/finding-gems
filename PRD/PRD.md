Product Requirement Document (PRD)
Product Name: Dualangka
Product Type: Creator Marketplace / AI-powered Website Catalog
Platform: Web (Responsive – Desktop & Mobile)
Tone & Branding: Professional, startup-style (black & white)
1. Product Overview
1.1 Problem Statement

High-quality, niche, and functional service websites (“hidden gems”)—especially those built by independent developers or AI-enabled creators—are difficult to discover through traditional search engines. These websites often solve very specific life, productivity, or professional problems but lack distribution, credibility, and monetization channels.

At the same time, users (individuals and businesses) struggle to efficiently discover, evaluate, and access such websites in one trusted place.

1.2 Solution

Dualangka is a curated, AI-powered web marketplace that functions as a catalog of service-based websites. It enables creators to showcase, promote, and monetize their websites, while allowing users to discover, evaluate, and purchase access to these services in a centralized platform.

Dualangka positions itself as:

A search engine for service websites and AI-powered tools.

1.3 Goals (MVP)

Validate demand for a curated service-website marketplace

Demonstrate real product thinking and execution (scholarship proof)

Enable basic creator monetization and buyer discovery within 1 month

16. Summary

Dualangka is positioned as a professional, curated marketplace for service websites, bridging the gap between creators building functional, AI-enabled tools and users seeking niche solutions. The MVP focuses on discovery, credibility, and basic monetization—intentionally keeping complexity low while maximizing learning and validation.
